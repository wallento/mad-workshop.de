MAD is now SMD

The Multicore Application Debugging (MAD) Workshop team is pleased to announce the merge with the S4D Workshop to form the new International Workshop on Solutions for Multicore Debug (SMD). In this way we strengthen our core idea to bring together the brightest minds in academia and industry on the subject of debugging in one place.

Please see the SMD web site for more details.
